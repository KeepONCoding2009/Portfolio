Entrepreneurship & Startups

The Vision of Legend's & Beyond

Alongside my journey in technology and AI, another passion has been steadily shaping my identity—entrepreneurship. For me, building projects was never just about coding or experimentation; it was about creating something that could grow, impact people, and stand the test of time. That mindset pushed me to step beyond being only a developer and start thinking like a builder of organizations.

One of my biggest ambitions is my company, Legend's. The vision behind Legend’s is bold and simple at the same time: to create a brand that represents excellence, creativity, and innovation at a global level. From the very beginning, I didn’t want it to be just another small project; I wanted it to be something that could evolve into one of the best companies in the world. Working on this vision has taught me how to think long term—about branding, positioning, and building something meaningful rather than chasing quick wins.

Entrepreneurship, however, has never been a solo journey for me. Collaboration has always played a huge role in how I build things. Together with my friends, I co-created CDX Animated Studio, a project born purely out of creativity and shared excitement. What started as a simple idea among friends quickly turned into a real initiative where we experimented with storytelling, visuals, and digital content. Building CDX taught me the importance of teamwork—how different skills and perspectives can come together to create something far more powerful than what one person could achieve alone.

These ventures were not just about launching names or projects; they were about learning how ideas evolve into realities. I experienced firsthand how challenging it can be to move from concept to execution—planning, dividing responsibilities, managing time, and staying consistent even when progress feels slow. Every small milestone, whether it was finalizing a concept, creating a prototype, or simply seeing people appreciate our work, reinforced my belief that entrepreneurship is a journey of persistence.

Over time, I have worked on several other initiatives as well—each one different in purpose but similar in spirit. Some focused on technology, some on creativity, and others on experimentation. Together, they shaped my mindset into one that constantly looks for opportunities to build and improve. Instead of waiting for the perfect moment, I learned to start with what I have and refine along the way.

What drives me most is the idea of impact. Whether it’s through a tech product, a creative studio, or a future global brand, my goal is to create things that inspire people and solve real problems. Entrepreneurship, to me, is not just about business—it’s about vision, courage, and the willingness to take risks in pursuit of something bigger than myself.

As I continue exploring new ideas and launching new ventures, each experience adds another layer to my understanding of leadership and innovation. The road ahead is still long, but with every project, I move one step closer to turning ambitious dreams into lasting realities.
